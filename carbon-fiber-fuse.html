Carbon Fiber Fuselage Mold

Figure 1: CAD Model of Bottom Fuselage Mold
In order to manufacture the composite skin of the plane, my project team CUAir uses coated high density foam molds. For our newest plane, I designed and manufactured the mold for the fuselage.

The design included tooling indents for quarter chord and the leading and trailing edge of the wing. It also included features to help with post processing and demolding.

For manufacturing, I independantly found and handled communciations with the CNC Company including ordering special CNC bits in order to create a certain geometry with an overhang. Sourced our large foam peices (), and handled delivery of materials to and from the location of the CNC.
